TigerSnatch helps Princeton students with course enrollment through its Subscriptions and Trades features.

With Subscriptions, students can receive an email and text message when spots open in a full course, so they can avoid repeatedly checking official Course Offerings. TigerSnatch Trades, a first-of-its-kind system at Princeton, connects students who want to switch into each other's full sections.

Give Feedback

TigerSnatch is actively maintained by the Princeton University Undergraduate Student Government (USG) TigerApps team. We thank Professor Robert Dondero and Sata Sengupta for their guidance on TigerSnatch.
